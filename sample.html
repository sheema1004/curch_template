“ABC’s Core Statement of Faith”

We believe in one true and eternal God, unchanging, unchangeable. We believe God is the Creator of all that exists in heaven and on earth., God has all power, all knowledge, all wisdom, and is due all glory, honor, and praise. All that comes to pass does so at the decree of God. All things will, in the end, result in the glory of God. (Genesis 1:1) We believe God exists eternally as three persons, Father, Son, and Holy Spirit (Luke 3:22, Matthew 28:19, 2 Corinthians 13:14 The Holy Spirit: He is God (Acts 5:3-4), He indwells and regenerates every believer (1 Corinthians 12:13, Romans 8:9), He fills each believer (Ephesians 5:18), He gives gifts to His children (Ephesians 4:4-7, 11:13, 1 Corinthians 12:4-7, 11, 14:26) Know more

We believe that God has spoken in the Scriptures, both Old and New Testaments, through the words of human authors. As the verbally inspired Word of God, the Bible is the complete revelation of His will for salvation, and the ultimate authority by which every realm of human knowledge and endeavor should be judged. Therefore, it is to be believed in all that it teaches, obeyed in all that it requires, and trusted in all that it promises. (II Timothy 3:16 & 2 Peter 1:21) Know more

We believe God exists eternally as three persons, Father, Son, and Holy Spirit (Luke 3:22, Matthew 28:19, 2 Corinthians 13:14 The Holy Spirit: He is God (Acts 5:3-4), He indwells and regenerates every believer (1 Corinthians 12:13, Romans 8:9), He fills each believer (Ephesians 5:18), He gives gifts to His children (Ephesians 4:4-7, 11:13, 1 Corinthians 12:4-7, 11, 14:26) Know more

Salvation is wholly of God by grace because of the redemptive work of Jesus Christ, the merit of His shed blood, and not based on human merit or works (John 1:12; Ephesians 1:7; 2:8-10; 1 Peter 1:18-19). Is complete and eternal salvation by God’s grace alone, received as the gift of God through the repentance of sin and personal faith in the Lord Jesus Christ and His finished work (Eph.2:8-9, Titus 3:5-7, 2Pet.3:9) Know more

We believe that man was created in the image of God.(Gen.1:26-28) Man rebelled against His Creator and fell into sin. As a result, man became spiritually dead, (Gen.3:2-24) totally unwilling and indeed incapable of seeking after God. God, from eternity past, having foreordained all things, joined a certain people to Christ Jesus, so that He might redeem them from their sin and in so doing bring glory to Himself. (Jn.3:3-5) Jesus Christ, the Son of God, died in the place of these elect people, providing full and complete forgiveness of sins by His death upon the cross. No other work can provide for forgiveness of sins, and no addition can be made to the completed and finished work of Christ. Know more

We believe Jesus Christ established His Church, which is made up of all the elect of God. His Church, as an obedient bride, listens to His Word as found in the Bible. All who believe in Christ are placed in His body, the Church. The local expressions of the Church are very important, and each believer should be actively involved in such a fellowship. One true church, the body, and bride of Christ (Eph.1:22-23). This body (the Church) is composed of all true believers of the present age, (1Cor.12:12-13) Know more

We believe that Christ is coming again to judge the living and the dead. This promise is found throughout the inspired Scriptures. Till His return, believers are to live lives that bring glory to God through Jesus Christ. The Church is to be busy doing the work of evangelism and discipleship, proclaiming the pure, uncompromised Gospel of Christ by teaching the Word of God. The personal, visible, and imminent return of Christ to remove His church from the earth (1Thes.4:16-17). Know more
